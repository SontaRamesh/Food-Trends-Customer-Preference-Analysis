# 🍽️ Food Trends & Understanding Customer Preferences in FB

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes food trends and customer preferences using the Food.com dataset. The analysis focuses on understanding user engagement, sentiment behavior, category performance, and strategic opportunities through interactive Power BI dashboards.

The project transforms raw recipe and review data into meaningful insights that can support product improvement and business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To analyze customer behavior and food trends in order to:
- Identify high-performing recipe categories  
- Understand customer satisfaction and sentiment patterns  
- Evaluate the impact of effort and health on preferences  
- Generate actionable insights for product and marketing strategy  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Power BI  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Power Query  
- CSV Dataset (Food.com)

## 📊 Dashboards & Analysis

### 1️⃣ Overview Dashboard
- Displays total recipes, total reviews, average rating, and preparation time  
- Shows that convenience-driven recipes generate higher engagement  

### 2️⃣ Category & Popularity Analysis
- Dessert is the most popular category  
- Engagement is concentrated in a few top categories  

### 3️⃣ Health & Preference Analysis
- HealthScore created using protein, fat, and sugar  
- Weak correlation between health and customer satisfaction  

### 4️⃣ Sentiment Analysis
- 88% of reviews are positive  
- Quick recipes receive higher positive sentiment  

### 5️⃣ Trend Analysis
- Engagement peaked around 2007–2008  
- Ratings remain stable over time  

### 6️⃣ Enhanced Performance Analysis
- Identifies star categories and hidden opportunities  
- Shows convenience drives engagement  

### 7️⃣ Product Performance Insights
- Maps reviews to sales and engagement to revenue  
- Dessert is the top-performing category  

### 8️⃣ Customer Behavior Segmentation
- Users are segmented into Loyal Fans, Critics, and Silent Users  
- Majority of users are Loyal Fans, indicating strong satisfaction  

### 9️⃣ Strategy & Innovation Dashboard
- Identifies top-performing categories and growth opportunities  
- Highlights hidden gem categories (high rating, low engagement)  
- Tracks sentiment trends over time  
- Supports marketing and product strategy decisions  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Customer engagement is highest for convenient and quick recipes  
- Dessert category dominates in engagement  
- Positive sentiment is very high (88%+)  
- Health factors have limited influence on user preference  
- High-rated but low-visibility categories present growth opportunities
